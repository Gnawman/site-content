The first keyboard I designed from the ground up, and an important stepping stone towards my current daily drivers. Currently on the second revision, the first try had a few issues - nothing that couldn't be worked around, but just annoying enough that I wanted to fix them. I use a slightly-modified footprint for the pro-micro, so the circuit board is flippable for each half but the microcontroller is face-up on both halves.

Designing the bevel was a lot of fun, since it meant an opportunity to learn pcb design, which was much less scary than expected.
